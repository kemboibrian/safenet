Alarm Systems


Safenet Installations provides intelligent alarm systems that deliver real-time protection against break-ins, fire, gas leaks, and unauthorized access. Our alarm solutions are designed to detect threats instantly and trigger loud sirens, push notifications, and automated emergency responses.


We deploy wired, wireless, and hybrid alarm technologies that suit both residential and commercial spaces. From entry sensors and vibration detectors to smoke alarms and glass-break sensors — every system is customized to your environment for maximum accuracy and coverage.


Service Features


Our alarm systems are smart, scalable, and compliant with modern security standards, giving you peace of mind around the clock.


  • Intrusion, fire, gas, and panic alarm integration
  • Smartphone alerts, SMS, and control panel access
  • Motion, door/window, vibration, and glass-break sensors
  • 24/7 monitoring with automated emergency response
  • Battery backup for power outages & GSM failover

Our licensed technicians carry out a full site assessment before installation to recommend the best system design for your needs. After installation, we provide comprehensive user training, remote support, and optional integration with CCTV and access control systems.


Whether for a home, office, school, or retail space, Safenet ensures you stay protected, connected, and in full control of your security system — even when you're away.

Safenet Installations delivers trusted security solutions — from CCTV and alarms to access control and electric fencing.
