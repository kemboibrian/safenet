Electric Fencing


Safenet Installations provides high-voltage electric fencing systems designed to prevent unauthorized access and intrusions across residential, commercial, and industrial properties. Our solutions act as both a physical and psychological barrier — deterring intruders before they reach your premises.


Our fences are equipped with powerful energizers, tamper detection, and real-time monitoring capabilities, offering round-the-clock perimeter security with minimal maintenance requirements.


Service Features


We install and maintain robust electric fences that comply with safety regulations and integrate with existing security systems.


  • High-voltage perimeter fencing systems
  • Energizers with shock deterrence & backup power
  • Intrusion alerts & alarm trigger on breach
  • Seamless integration with CCTV & alarm systems
  • Compliant with international safety standards

Our experienced team handles everything — from site assessment and energizer placement to full system wiring, signage, and testing. We also offer annual maintenance plans to ensure continued performance and reliability.


With Safenet’s electric fencing, you gain a powerful first line of defense, protecting your property perimeter with efficiency, reliability, and peace of mind.

Safenet Installations delivers trusted security solutions — from CCTV and alarms to access control and electric fencing.
