Access Control Systems


At Safenet Installations, we provide modern access control solutions that allow you to manage and monitor who enters your premises, when, and how. From biometric scanners to RFID card readers and keypad systems, our access control setups are ideal for securing offices, schools, homes, data centers, and high-risk facilities.


Whether you need a standalone system for a single door or an enterprise-level solution across multiple locations, our systems are designed to offer flexibility, scalability, and full audit trails — all from a secure platform you control.


Service Features


Control access with confidence using smart technology that’s built for today's security landscape.


  • Biometric (fingerprint, facial) & RFID card access
  • PIN code/keypad entry & mobile-based unlocking
  • Time-based and role-based access permissions
  • Real-time entry logs and activity reports
  • Seamless integration with CCTV & alarm systems

Our systems can be cloud-managed or hosted on-premises depending on your needs, with remote access and centralized dashboards for security administrators. We also offer advanced features like interlocking doors, anti-passback, and visitor management.


With Safenet, your property’s access points become intelligent, traceable, and virtually impenetrable — ensuring only the right people get in, at the right time.

Safenet Installations delivers trusted security solutions — from CCTV and alarms to access control and electric fencing.
