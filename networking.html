Professional Networking Solutions


At Safenet Installations, we deliver high-performance networking infrastructure tailored for both residential and commercial environments. Our structured cabling systems and wireless solutions are designed for speed, security, and future scalability — ensuring your business operations or smart home systems run seamlessly.


Whether you're setting up a small office LAN or deploying enterprise-grade networks across multiple sites, we ensure reliable data flow, minimized downtime, and secure connectivity — aligned with today’s IT and cybersecurity standards.


Service Features


Our certified networking engineers apply best practices and the latest technologies to ensure optimal performance and long-term reliability.


  • Structured cabling (Cat6/Cat6a, fiber optics)
  • Enterprise Wi-Fi planning and installation
  • Network configuration & optimization
  • Secure router, firewall & switch setup
  • Server room design and rack installation
  • 24/7 support and remote troubleshooting

We work with leading brands such as Cisco, Ubiquiti, TP-Link, and Mikrotik to deploy networks that support VoIP, video surveillance, cloud systems, and IoT devices without lag or security concerns.


Our end-to-end networking services include site surveys, structured installations, device configuration, performance testing, and comprehensive support packages.


With Safenet, you're not just getting cables — you're investing in a connected future built for reliability, speed, and growth.
